Scale House · The Creative System

One winner → ten winners.

Most agencies find a winning ad and ride it until it dies. We do two things they don't: we diagnose exactly why it won, then systematically multiply it before it burns out. Here's the system on a whiteboard.

THE PROBLEM
A winner with no diagnosis = a coin flip. When it dies, you can't rebuild it — and the account collapses with it.
↓  so first, we diagnose
ACT 1 · DIAGNOSE

We don't guess why it worked. We score it.

A 6-question pass on every winner (Schwartz awareness + Bencivenga's 4-component score). Honest math, not vibes.
1Angle name — 3–5 words, e.g. "It's parasites, not fats"
2Awareness level — where the reader enters → exits
3Belief shift — old belief out, new belief in
4The "I want" — the one core desire, in their words
5Identification — the self-image they step into
6Bencivenga score — /100, the 4 components below
The 4-component scorecard — 25 pts each

The score IS the iteration brief. A winner can score low — that just tells us exactly what to add next. Proof at 5/25 → next test adds testimonials. Offer at 0/25 → next test adds a guarantee. Every test is briefed by data.
